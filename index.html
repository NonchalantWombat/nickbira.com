Body of Work

❮ ❯

My name is Dr. Nicholas Bira, and I am a robotics researcher living and working in the greater Boston Area. With a PhD in Robotics from Oregon State University, my research has explored hardware-based methods for controlling compliant robotic mechanisms with smart materials, specifically via the use of magnetic fluids and electrompermanent magnets.

I am actively pursuing positions in robotics and R&D. I have experience in all phases of the research process, from writing successful grant applications, to managing teams of student researchers, to publishing and presenting work in top journals and in leading robotics conferences. I am driven to solve complex problems through collaboration and developing new skills.

Outside of my work career, my passions include cooking and baking, as well as D&D and making things. I ran a resin dice Etsy shop for some time, and I've collaborated on the design of products and engineering methods (artisanal candles with Cantrip Candles from Los Angeles in partiulcar.)
